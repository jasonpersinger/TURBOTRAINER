GS4 TurboTrainer
bulk skill training for gemstone iv

Install

⚡ GS4 TurboTrainer
Drag this button to your bookmarks bar.
If the bar is hidden: Ctrl+Shift+B / ⌘+Shift+B

Usage

  1. Log in and open the GS4 skill trainer
  2. Click ⚡ GS4 TurboTrainer in your bookmarks bar
  3. +5, +10, -5, -10 buttons appear next to every skill
  4. Submit your training as normal when done
