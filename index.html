> HOW TO USE
01
Drag the button above to your browser's bookmarks bar. If the bar isn't visible, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows/Linux) or ⌘+Shift+B (Mac).
02
Log in to GemStone IV and open the Character Manager skill trainer.
03
Click ⚡ GS4 TURBOTRAINER in your bookmarks bar.
04
New +5 +10 -5 -10 buttons appear next to every skill. Click them to train in bulk. The original +1 / -1 buttons still work normally.
05
When done, submit your training as usual. TURBOTRAINER only adds buttons — it doesn't change how the page saves.
